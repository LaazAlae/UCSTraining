Cash Application Specialist Training

Your Role Overview

As a Cash Application Specialist at UCS, you will be responsible for processing various types of payments received by the company. UCS receives payments through multiple channels including credit cards, checks, wire transfers, and ACH transactions.

Your primary focus will be on processing credit card and check payments, which are categorized into two main types:

  • Renewals (R invoices): Recurring service payments and contract renewals
  • Projects (P invoices): One-time project payments and new service implementations

Your main responsibility is to accurately enter these payments into our system, ensuring proper categorization and timely processing to maintain our cash flow and client relationships.

Credit Card Payment Processing

Step-by-Step Process

1

Open Projects

Launch the UCS project management system from your desktop or browser bookmark.

2

Login with Your Credentials

Enter your assigned username and password to access the system.

3

Navigate to Cash Receipts

In the left sidebar menu, locate and click on "Cash Receipts" to access the payment processing module.

4

Create Receipt Batch

On the main page, click "Create Receipt Batch" to begin processing credit card payments.

5

Enter Batch Name

In the batch name field, enter: MM/DD/YYYY-CC-DEP
Replace the date placeholders with the actual deposit date (e.g., 03/15/2024-CC-DEP)

6

Enter Deposit Date

Select the deposit date from the calendar picker or enter it manually in MM/DD/YYYY format.

7

Copy to Batch Description

Copy the exact content from the batch name field and paste it into the batch description field.

8

Save Batch

Click the "Save" button to create the batch. The system will generate a unique batch ID.

9

Add Receipt

Click "Add Receipt" to begin entering individual credit card payment details.

10

Enter Payment Details

Complete all required fields including amount, customer information, and invoice references.

Check Payment Processing

Check Processing Overview

Check payments are separated into two distinct batch types:

  • R Batches: For Renewal invoice payments
  • P Batches: For Project invoice payments

Important Rule: If a check contains multiple invoices but only 1 Project (P) invoice, the entire check should go into the P batch.
